Excel Fantasy Basketball Project

An excel project I put together to track the standard deviation of NBA Player's stats in multiple categories. Player replacement value for injured players, variance in players, and comparison to other fantasy teams are also tracked.

    Above is the matchup calculator used in the excel sheet. For every weekly matchup, the sheet will calculate ever rostered player's stats from the entire season and the past 2 weeks, count their total games for the week and predict the total points the player will score in the week. It does the same for your opponent's team and gives you multiple helpful tools. Those tools include games per day (10 max), points per day, total games, overlap games, percent chance to win, and estimated total.
